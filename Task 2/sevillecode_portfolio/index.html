About Me

I have been working with HTML since 2010 when I was unexpectedly volunteered to build a website for a club. At the time I knew nothing about web design, so I set about learning everything I could about it. The more I learned, the more I loved it, and I went on to study web design in college. In May 2013, I graduated from Laramie County Community College with an Associate of Applied Sciences' Degree in Computer Information Systems with certificates in web design, computer security, and PC repair. In May 2019, I graduated from Arizona State University with a Bachelor of Applied Science Degree in Internet and Web Development.

With regards to the writing, I've created stories in one form or another since I was 11, and I later branched out into nonfiction/opinion/reviews. My articles have been featured on MoviePilot, CultBox, and WhatCulture.
